<p><p>3D printed jewelry market types can be broadly categorized into SLA, SLS, DLP, FDM technologies, and other markets. SLA technology uses a laser to cure liquid resin, resulting in detailed and precise pieces. SLS technology involves sintering powdered material using a laser, ideal for complex designs. DLP technology uses a projector to cure resin layer by layer, providing faster production times. FDM technology melts plastic filament to create jewelry, offering affordability. Other markets may include emerging technologies or niche applications within the 3D printed jewelry industry.</p></p>
