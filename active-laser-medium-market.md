<p><p>Active laser medium can be classified into four main types based on their physical state: solid, liquid, gaseous, or plasma. Solid laser mediums can be either crystalline or amorphous, and typically have high energy densities. Liquid laser mediums offer ease of pumping and cooling. Gaseous laser mediums are commonly used in gas lasers and have low energy densities. Plasma laser mediums are highly ionized gases that can generate intense laser beams. Each type has specific applications and benefits in the active laser medium market.</p></p>
