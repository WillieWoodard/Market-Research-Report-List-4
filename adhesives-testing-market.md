<p><p>Adhesives testing market consists of various types of tests to evaluate the quality and performance of adhesives. Peel strength testing measures the force required to peel two bonded surfaces apart. Tack strength testing assesses the ability of an adhesive to adhere quickly upon contact with a surface. Shear strength testing evaluates the ability of an adhesive to withstand perpendicular forces causing it to slide or shear along the surface. These tests help manufacturers ensure their adhesives meet specific performance requirements for various applications.</p></p>
