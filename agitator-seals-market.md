<p><ul><li>Packing Seal</li><li>Mechanical Seal</li><li>Magnetic Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agitator seals are essential for preventing leakage in industrial processes. The three main types are packing seals, mechanical seals, and magnetic drive seals. Packing seals use compressed packing material to create a secure seal around the shaft. Mechanical seals utilize rotating and stationary components to prevent leakage. Magnetic drive seals employ magnetic force to transmit torque and create a seal. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on factors such as operating conditions and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
