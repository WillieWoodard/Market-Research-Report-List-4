<p><strong>The ANC Headset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Cup (Circumaural)</li><li>On-Ear (Supra Aural)</li><li>In-Ear (Intraaural)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ANC headsets come in three main types: Full Cup (Circumaural), On-Ear (Supra Aural), and In-Ear (Intraaural). Full Cup headsets cover the ears completely, providing maximum noise cancellation and sound isolation. On-Ear headsets rest on the ears, offering a balance between noise cancellation and comfort. In-Ear headsets are small, lightweight, and fit directly into the ear canal for minimal noise cancellation but maximum portability. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of users.</p></p>
