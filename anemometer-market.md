<p><strong>The Anemometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Velocity Anemometer</li><li>Pressure Anemometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anemometer market consists of two main types: Velocity Anemometer and Pressure Anemometer. </p><p>Velocity Anemometers measure the speed and direction of the wind using various techniques such as cup, vane, or hot-wire anemometers. They are commonly used in weather stations, HVAC systems, and environmental monitoring.</p><p>Pressure Anemometers, on the other hand, measure air pressure changes caused by wind speed, providing indirect measurements of wind speed. They are often used in aviation, meteorology, and research applications. Both types play a crucial role in monitoring and analyzing wind conditions.</p></p>
