<p><p>Ant control market consists of three main types: spray, powder, and bait. Spray products are typically used for immediate results by directly targeting ants on contact. Powder products are sprinkled in ant trails or nest entrances to kill ants when they come into contact with it. Bait products attract ants to consume poison, taking it back to the colony to kill the entire population. Each of these market types serve a different purpose in effectively controlling ant infestations.</p></p>
