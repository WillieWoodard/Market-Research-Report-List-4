<p><ul><li>Unit -type Machine</li><li>Central Impression Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic flexographic printers can be divided into two main market types: unit-type machines and central impression machines. Unit-type machines have separate printing units, allowing for greater flexibility in printing multiple colors and designs. Central impression machines have a single central drum that all colors pass through, resulting in higher print quality and registration accuracy. Both types of machines are commonly used in the printing industry, with unit-type machines being more popular for smaller printing jobs and central impression machines being preferred for larger, high-quality printing projects.</p></p>
