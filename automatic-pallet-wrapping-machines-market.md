<p><p>Automatic pallet wrapping machines come in two main types: single-arm and double-arm. Single-arm machines have one rotating arm that wraps the pallet with stretch film, while double-arm machines have two rotating arms that work simultaneously to expedite the wrapping process. Single-arm machines are typically more compact and cost-effective, making them suitable for small-scale operations. On the other hand, double-arm machines offer higher efficiency and faster wrapping speeds, making them ideal for larger-scale production facilities.</p></p>
