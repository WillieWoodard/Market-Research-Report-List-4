<p><p>Battery isolators are devices used to prevent electrical systems in vehicles from draining the main battery. There are two main types of battery isolators - diode type and relay type. Diode type isolators use semiconductor diodes to prevent current flow between batteries, while relay type isolators use electromechanical relays to control the flow of current. Both types of isolators are essential for maintaining the integrity of vehicle electrical systems and ensuring the proper functioning of multiple battery setups.</p></p>
