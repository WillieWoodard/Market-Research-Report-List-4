<p><p>Carton erectors are available in two main market types: semi-automatic and automatic. Semi-automatic carton erectors require some level of manual input from operators, such as loading the flat carton into the machine and sealing it after it has been erected. On the other hand, automatic carton erectors are fully automated and can erect, fold, and seal cartons without the need for manual intervention. Both types offer efficiency and convenience in packaging operations, with automatic carton erectors being more suitable for high-volume production environments.</p></p>
