<p><p>Ceramic sputtering targets are used in physical vapor deposition processes for thin film deposition in various industries. Planar targets are flat surfaces used in sputtering machines, while rotary targets are cylindrical in shape and rotate during the deposition process. Planar targets are often used for large area deposition, while rotary targets are preferred for higher material utilization efficiency and better film quality in some applications. Both types cater to the needs of different industries and processes in the ceramic sputtering target market.</p></p>
