<p><strong>The Chlor-alkali Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Membrane Method</li><li>Diaphragm Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chlor-alkali equipment market includes two main types: Ion Exchange Membrane Method and Diaphragm Method. In the Ion Exchange Membrane Method, salt is dissolved in water to produce chlorine, caustic soda, and hydrogen. This method is known for its high efficiency and low energy consumption. On the other hand, the Diaphragm Method involves the electrolysis of salt using a diaphragm to separate chlorine and caustic soda. This method is preferred for its ease of operation and lower maintenance requirements.</p></p>
