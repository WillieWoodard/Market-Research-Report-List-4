<p><ul><li>SVC Type</li><li>AVC Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud video conferencing market can be categorized into SVC (Scalable Video Coding) type and AVC (Advanced Video Coding) type. SVC type allows for greater flexibility in adapting to varying network conditions by encoding video streams into multiple layers. This enables smoother video playback and better quality even under poor network conditions. On the other hand, AVC type provides high-quality video transmission with higher compression rates, making it suitable for high-speed networks. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the video conferencing market.</p></p>
