<p><p>CNC 2D Laser Cutting Machines are classified into two main types: CO2 2D Laser Machines and Fiber 2D Laser Machines. CO2 machines use a carbon dioxide laser to cut materials like wood, acrylic, and plastic, while Fiber machines use a fiber laser for cutting tougher materials like metal. Each type of machine has its own advantages and applications, with CO2 machines being more cost-effective for lighter materials and Fiber machines offering higher precision and faster cutting speeds for metal materials.</p></p>
