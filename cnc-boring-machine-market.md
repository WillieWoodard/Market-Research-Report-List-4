<p><p>CNC Boring Machine market is divided into two main types - Vertical Boring Machine and Horizontal Boring Machine. Vertical Boring Machines are used for operations where the workpiece is held vertically and the cutting tool rotates on a vertical axis. They are suitable for machining cylindrical workpieces. On the other hand, Horizontal Boring Machines are used for larger workpieces where the cutting tool is mounted horizontally on a spindle. They are ideal for precision drilling and boring operations on large and heavy components.</p></p>
