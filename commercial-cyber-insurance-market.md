<p><p>Commercial Cyber Insurance can be divided into two main types: stand-alone Cyber Insurance and packaged Cyber Insurance. Stand-alone Cyber Insurance policies are specifically tailored to cover cyber risks and liabilities only, providing comprehensive coverage for data breaches, cyber-attacks, and other cyber-related incidents. Packaged Cyber Insurance, on the other hand, is often bundled with other insurance policies such as general liability or property insurance, offering a more cost-effective solution for businesses looking to protect themselves against cyber threats alongside their other commercial insurance needs.</p></p>
