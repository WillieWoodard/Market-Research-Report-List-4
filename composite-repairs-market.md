Structural repairs involve fixing critical components like load-bearing structures, requiring high strength and durability. Semi-Structural repairs address moderate damage to non-critical components without compromising on overall performance. Cosmetic repairs focus on surface-level damage, improving appearance and aesthetics. Each market type caters to specific repair needs and plays a crucial role in ensuring the safety and longevity of composite materials.</p></p>
