<p><p>Compression coil springs are categorized into various types based on their design and application. Conical coil springs have a tapered shape, providing varying levels of compression resistance. Variable pitch coil springs have coils with different spacing along the length of the spring, offering variable stiffness. Barrel coil springs have a larger diameter in the middle, providing greater flexibility in handling heavy loads. Ordinary coil springs have a uniform coil pitch and diameter. Other types of coil springs include elliptical and progressive rate springs, offering specialized performance characteristics.</p></p>
