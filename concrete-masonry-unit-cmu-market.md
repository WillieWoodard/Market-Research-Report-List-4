<p><p>Concrete Masonry Unit (CMU) market includes two main types: Load Bearing Block and Non-Load Bearing Block. Load Bearing Blocks are designed to carry structural loads and support the weight of the building, making them essential for the construction of walls and other load-bearing structures. On the other hand, Non-Load Bearing Blocks are used in partitions, infill panels, and decorative facades where structural support is not required. Both types serve different purposes in the construction industry and cater to different structural needs.</p></p>
