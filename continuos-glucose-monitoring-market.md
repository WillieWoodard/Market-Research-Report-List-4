<p><ul><li>Retrospective CGM (r-CGM)</li><li>Real-time continuous glucose monitoring (RT-CGM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) systems can be classified into two main types - Retrospective CGM (r-CGM) and Real-time continuous glucose monitoring (RT-CGM). </p><p>Retrospective CGM involves the review of stored glucose data after a certain period of time, typically downloaded from the device and reviewed by healthcare providers during clinic visits. </p><p>Real-time CGM provides continuous glucose readings in real-time, allowing users to track their glucose levels throughout the day and receive alerts for high or low blood sugar levels. </p><p>Both types of CGM systems offer valuable insights for better diabetes management.</p></p>
