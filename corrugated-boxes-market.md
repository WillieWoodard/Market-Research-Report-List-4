<p><p>Corrugated boxes market types include single corrugated, double corrugated, and triple corrugated. Single corrugated boxes have one layer of fluting between two linerboards, providing basic strength and cushioning. Double corrugated boxes have two layers of fluting for added protection, making them suitable for heavier or fragile items. Triple corrugated boxes have three layers of fluting, offering the highest level of durability and strength, ideal for transporting extremely heavy or delicate items. Each type caters to different packaging needs based on the weight and fragility of the contents.</p></p>
