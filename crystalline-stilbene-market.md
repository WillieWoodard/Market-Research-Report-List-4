<p><p>Crystalline Stilbene has two main market types: (E)-Stilbene and (Z)-Stilbene. (E)-Stilbene is a type of stilbene compound where the two phenyl rings are on opposite sides of the central double bond, resulting in a straight molecular structure. (Z)-Stilbene, on the other hand, has the two phenyl rings on the same side of the double bond, leading to a bent molecular structure. Both types have unique properties and applications in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, plastics, and cosmetics.</p></p>
