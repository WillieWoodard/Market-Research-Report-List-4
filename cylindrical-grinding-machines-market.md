<p><p>Cylindrical grinding machines can be classified into three main types based on their control systems. CNC (Computer Numerical Control) machines use computer programming to control the grinding process, resulting in high precision and automation. Manually-controlled machines require operators to adjust and control the grinding operation by hand, offering more flexibility but lower efficiency. PLC (Programmable Logic Control) machines use pre-set commands to control the grinding process, offering a middle ground between CNC and manual control options.</p></p>
