<p><p>Dead weight testers are devices used to calibrate pressure measuring instruments. There are three main types of dead weight testers: Hydraulic, Pneumatic, and Others. Hydraulic dead weight testers use hydraulic fluid to generate pressure, while pneumatic testers use compressed air. The "Others" category includes electronic dead weight testers. Each type has its own advantages and applications, with hydraulic testers typically used for high-pressure applications, pneumatic testers for lower pressures, and electronic testers for modern, digital calibration processes.</p></p>
