<p><strong>The Differential Scanning Calorimeter (DSC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Flux DSC</li><li>Power-compensation DSC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Differential Scanning Calorimeter (DSC) is a tool used to measure the heat flow into or out of a sample as it undergoes a controlled temperature change. There are two main types of DSC in the market: Heat Flux DSC and Power-Compensation DSC. </p><p>Heat Flux DSC measures the temperature difference between the sample and reference material to determine the heat flow, while Power-Compensation DSC uses a feedback system to maintain the sample and reference at the same temperature, with the heat needed to do so indicating any thermal transitions in the sample.</p></p>
