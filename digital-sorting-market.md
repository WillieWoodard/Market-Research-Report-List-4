<p><p>Digital sorting markets include cameras, lasers, NIR sorters, hyperspectral cameras, and combined sorters. Cameras use visual inspection to detect defects in products. Lasers utilize light beams to identify and sort objects. NIR sorters use near-infrared technology to analyze the chemical composition of materials. Hyperspectral cameras use advanced imaging techniques to provide detailed information about the properties of objects. Combined sorters incorporate multiple technologies to achieve higher accuracy and efficiency in sorting processes. Each of these technologies caters to specific needs and requirements in the sorting industry.</p></p>
