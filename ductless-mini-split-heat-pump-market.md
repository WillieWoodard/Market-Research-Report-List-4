<p><p>The ductless mini split heat pump market consists of two main types: outdoor compressor units and indoor air-handling units. The outdoor compressor unit is responsible for transferring heat from the outside air to the indoor unit through refrigerant lines. The indoor air-handling unit then distributes the heated or cooled air throughout the space. This setup allows for more flexibility in controlling the temperature in different rooms without the need for ductwork, making it a popular choice for residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
