<p><p>Dyslipidemia drugs market can be divided into three major types: Statins, Cholesterol Absorption Inhibitors, and Others. Statins work by reducing the production of cholesterol in the liver. Cholesterol Absorption Inhibitors block the absorption of cholesterol from the intestines. Other drugs in this market include fibrates, bile acid sequestrants, and PCSK9 inhibitors, which work through different mechanisms to lower cholesterol levels. Each type of drug has its own unique way of addressing dyslipidemia and reducing the risk of cardiovascular disease.</p></p>
