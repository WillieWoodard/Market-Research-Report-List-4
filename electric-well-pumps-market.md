<p><p>Electric well pumps are commonly categorized into two main types based on their application: Openwell pumps and Borewell pumps. Openwell pumps are designed for use in shallow wells or open wells where the water level is relatively close to the surface. On the other hand, Borewell pumps are specifically designed for deep wells or boreholes where the water level is significantly deeper underground. Each type of pump is tailored to meet the specific requirements of different well depths and water extraction needs.</p></p>
