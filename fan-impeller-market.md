<p><p>Fan impellers are categorized into three main types based on their design: closed impeller, semi-open impeller, and open impeller. Closed impellers are fully enclosed and provide high efficiency and performance. Semi-open impellers have one side open, allowing for easier maintenance and cleaning. Open impellers have all blades exposed and are commonly used in applications where solids need to pass through. Each type of impeller is suited for different types of fan applications based on the specific requirements for efficiency, maintenance, and performance.</p></p>
