<p><p>Fiber Bragg Grating Reflectors are commonly used in two types of fibers: Single Mode Fiber and Double-Clad Fiber. Single Mode Fiber is designed to carry light propagation along a single path, making it ideal for long-distance communication. On the other hand, Double-Clad Fiber has an inner core for light propagation and an outer cladding for pump light delivery, making it suitable for fiber lasers and amplifiers. Both types of fibers have their specific applications in the Fiber Bragg Grating Reflector market.</p></p>
