<p><p>Fiducial markers are used in medical imaging to precisely locate specific areas within the body during procedures such as radiation therapy. Polymer-based markers are generally biocompatible and offer good visibility on imaging scans. Metal-based markers, often made of materials like gold or platinum, provide excellent visibility and durability. Other markers may include ceramic or hybrid materials that offer unique benefits for specific imaging applications.</p></p>
