<p><p>Fluid-immersed Distribution Transformers come in two main types - Single Phase and Three Phase. Single Phase transformers are designed for smaller residential and commercial applications, while Three Phase transformers are used in larger industrial and commercial settings. Single Phase transformers have one primary and one secondary winding, while Three Phase transformers have three primary and three secondary windings. Both types of transformers are immersed in a cooling fluid, such as oil, to dissipate heat and enhance performance and efficiency.</p></p>
