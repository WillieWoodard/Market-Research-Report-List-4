<p><p>Food dehydrators for small businesses come in two main types: vertical airflow and horizontal airflow. Vertical airflow dehydrators have the heating element and fan positioned at the bottom or top of the unit, allowing for even circulation of air throughout the trays. Horizontal airflow dehydrators, on the other hand, have the fan and heating element located on the back or side of the unit, providing consistent airflow across the trays. Both types are suitable for small businesses looking to efficiently dehydrate various food products.</p></p>
