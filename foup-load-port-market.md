<p><ul><li>Motor Drive</li><li>Air Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FOUP Load Port market types include Motor Drive and Air Drive designs. Motor Drive load ports are equipped with electric motors to drive the movement of FOUPs, providing precise control and stability. On the other hand, Air Drive load ports rely on air pressure to push and pull FOUPs into position, offering a simpler and potentially more cost-effective solution. Both market types cater to different customer requirements, with Motor Drive load ports suited for high precision applications and Air Drive load ports offering a more straightforward approach.</p></p>
