<p><p>FPV Drone Goggles are available in various market types based on resolution - QVGA (Quarter VGA), VGA (Video Graphics Array), SVGA (Super Video Graphics Array), and FWVGA (Full Wide VGA). QVGA offers the lowest resolution, followed by VGA, SVGA, and FWVGA which provide progressively higher resolutions. QVGA displays an image resolution of 320x240 pixels, VGA displays 640x480 pixels, SVGA displays 800x600 pixels, and FWVGA displays 854x480 pixels. Higher resolution goggles offer clearer and more detailed images for a better FPV experience.</p></p>
