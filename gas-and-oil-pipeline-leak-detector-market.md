<p><strong>The Gas and Oil Pipeline Leak Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mass-Volume Balance Method</li><li>Acoustic/Ultrasonic</li><li>Fiber Optics</li><li>Vapor Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas and oil pipeline leak detectors utilize various methods to identify leaks in pipelines. The Mass-Volume Balance Method calculates leaks by measuring the difference between gas injected into the pipeline and gas consumed at the other end. Acoustic/Ultrasonic detectors use sound waves to detect leaks, while Fiber Optics utilize light signals to identify changes in pressure caused by leaks. Vapor Sensing detectors detect leaks by analyzing the composition of gas in the pipeline. These methods provide different ways to effectively monitor and prevent leaks in gas and oil pipelines.</p></p>
