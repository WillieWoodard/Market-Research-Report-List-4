<p><p>Genetic testing services market includes prenatal testing, which involves screening the fetus for genetic disorders before birth. Newborn screening is performed on newborns to detect certain genetic conditions early on. Predictive and presymptomatic testing is done to assess the risk of developing a genetic disorder later in life. Pharmacogenomic testing analyzes how an individual's genetic makeup may affect their response to certain medications. These different types of genetic testing services help in early detection, prevention, and personalized treatment of genetic conditions.</p></p>
