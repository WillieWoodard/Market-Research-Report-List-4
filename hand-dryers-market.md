<p><p>The hand dryers market offers two main types: warm air hand dryers and jet-air hand dryers. Warm air hand dryers use heated air to evaporate moisture on hands, offering a slower drying time but are more energy efficient. On the other hand, jet-air hand dryers use a powerful blast of air to quickly dry hands in a matter of seconds, but are typically more energy-intensive. Both types of hand dryers offer a hygienic and environmentally friendly alternative to paper towels.</p></p>
