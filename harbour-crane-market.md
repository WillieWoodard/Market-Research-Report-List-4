<p><p>Harbour cranes can be categorized into two main types based on their mobility: mobile and fixed cranes. Mobile cranes are designed to be easily transported and moved around within a harbor or port, providing flexibility and versatility in handling cargo. On the other hand, fixed cranes are permanently installed in one location and primarily used for heavy-duty lifting operations.
