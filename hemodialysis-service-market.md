<p><p>Hemodialysis services encompass various types of treatments, including regular hemodialysis, online hemodiafiltration, and other specialized methods. Regular hemodialysis involves the use of a dialysis machine to filter blood through a dialyzer, removing waste and excess fluids. Online hemodiafiltration is a more advanced form of treatment that combines hemodialysis with additional filtration mechanisms, providing more efficient blood purification. Other specialized methods may include peritoneal dialysis or home hemodialysis, offering patients different options based on their needs and preferences.</p></p>
