<p><p>High Temperature Sensors market consists of various types such as Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs), Thermocouples, and Semiconductor-based Sensors. RTDs measure temperature by changes in electrical resistance, providing accurate results over a wide temperature range. Thermocouples generate a voltage signal based on the temperature difference between two dissimilar metals, suitable for high-temperature applications. Semiconductor-based sensors utilize semiconductor materials to measure temperature, offering fast response times and high accuracy. Each type caters to different needs and requirements in the market for high-temperature sensing applications.</p></p>
