<p><ul><li>Singal Axis</li><li>Triaxial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IEPE accelerometers come in two main types - single axis and triaxial. Single axis accelerometers measure acceleration in one direction, while triaxial accelerometers measure acceleration in three directions simultaneously. Single axis accelerometers are often used in applications where only one direction of acceleration needs to be measured, such as in vertical vibration monitoring. Triaxial accelerometers, on the other hand, are used in applications where acceleration in multiple directions needs to be monitored, providing a more comprehensive picture of the system's vibrations.</p></p>
