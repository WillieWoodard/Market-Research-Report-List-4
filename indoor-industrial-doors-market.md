<p><p>The indoor industrial doors market includes various types such as swinging, sliding, bypass, folding, revolving, and others. Swinging doors operate on hinges and open in one direction. Sliding doors slide horizontally along a track. Bypass doors slide past each other. Folding doors fold and stack to the side. Revolving doors consist of multiple panels that rotate around a central axis. Other types may include vertical lift, roll-up, and high-speed doors, each offering unique benefits for industrial applications.</p></p>
