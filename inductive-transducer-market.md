<p><p>Inductive transducers are classified into different types based on their mechanisms of operation. The electromagnetic transducer uses the principle of electromagnetic induction to convert mechanical displacement into an electrical signal. Piezoelectric transducers utilize the piezoelectric effect to generate an electrical charge in response to mechanical stress. Other types of transducers include capacitive, resistive, and optical transducers, each using a unique method to convert physical quantities into electrical signals. These variations in transducer technology cater to diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
