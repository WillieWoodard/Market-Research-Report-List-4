<p><p>The industrial knitting equipment market can be categorized into two main types: semi-automatic and automatic machines. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention from the operator, such as loading yarn and monitoring the process, while automatic machines are fully automated and require minimal human involvement. Semi-automatic machines are usually preferred for smaller-scale production where flexibility is needed, while automatic machines are more suitable for larger-scale operations where efficiency and speed are paramount.
