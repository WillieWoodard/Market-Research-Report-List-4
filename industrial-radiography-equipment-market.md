<p><p>Industrial radiography equipment market includes film-based radiography and digital radiography. Film-based radiography uses x-ray film to capture images, which are then developed and examined for defects. Digital radiography, on the other hand, uses digital sensors to capture images, which can be instantly viewed and analyzed. Digital radiography offers advantages such as faster image processing, lower radiation exposure, and easier storage and sharing of images. Both types of radiography play a crucial role in non-destructive testing in various industries.</p></p>
