<p><p>IV Extension Set Market is categorized into Single-Lumen (SL) and Double-Lumen (DL) sets. Single-Lumen sets consist of one pathway for fluid administration, commonly used for routine infusion therapy. On the other hand, Double-Lumen sets have two separate pathways, allowing for simultaneous infusion of two different fluids or medications. Both types of sets play a crucial role in delivering medication, blood, or fluids to patients efficiently and safely during medical procedures and treatments.</p></p>
