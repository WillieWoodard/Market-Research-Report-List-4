<p><p>Lapping machines are classified into three main types based on their automation level: Semi-Automatic Type, Fully Automatic Type, and CNC Type. Semi-Automatic machines require manual loading and unloading of workpieces, with some automation during the lapping process. Fully Automatic machines operate independently once the workpieces are loaded. CNC Type machines are the most advanced, with computer-controlled precision in both lapping and tool movement. Each type caters to different levels of automation and precision in lapping operations.</p></p>
