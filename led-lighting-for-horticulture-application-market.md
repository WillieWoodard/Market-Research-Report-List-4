<p><p>LED lighting for horticulture applications can be divided into two main market types: LED lamps and LED luminaires. LED lamps are individual light sources that can be used for specific applications in horticulture, such as providing supplemental lighting for plants. LED luminaires, on the other hand, are complete lighting fixtures that include the LED light source, housing, and often control systems. These are commonly used for larger scale horticulture operations where uniform lighting is needed across a growing area.</p></p>
