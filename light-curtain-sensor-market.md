<p><strong>The Light Curtain Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Light Curtain Sensors</li><li>Diffuse Light Curtain Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflective Light Curtain Sensors are devices that use a transmitter and receiver to detect objects within a certain range by reflecting light off the object back to the sensor. Diffuse Light Curtain Sensors, on the other hand, use a single transmitter and receiver to detect objects by measuring the amount of light received. Reflective sensors are more suited for longer distances and precise detection, while diffuse sensors are better for detecting objects regardless of their size, shape, or color within a shorter range.</p></p>
