<p><p>Lithium-ion Capacitors are available in two main types: Radial Type and Laminating Type. Radial Type capacitors are cylindrical in shape and are suitable for applications where space is a constraint. On the other hand, Laminating Type capacitors are flat and thin, making them ideal for compact devices. Both types offer high energy density and long cycle life, making them popular choices in various industries such as consumer electronics, automotive, and renewable energy.</p></p>
