<p><p>Low leakage diodes are semiconductor devices that have a very low reverse leakage current compared to standard diodes. In the market, there are two main types of low leakage diodes: monolithic low leakage diodes and monolithic dual-channel low leakage diodes. Monolithic low leakage diodes are single-channel diodes that provide low leakage current for applications requiring high precision. On the other hand, monolithic dual-channel low leakage diodes offer two channels in a single device, making them suitable for applications that require increased functionality and space-saving design.</p></p>
