<p><p>Low Voltage Switchboards refer to electrical distribution panels used to control and distribute electricity in buildings. Single Front switchboards have all their components accessible from only one side, while Double Front switchboards have components accessible from both sides. Single Front switchboards are often used in smaller installations where space is limited, while Double Front switchboards are commonly used in larger installations to allow for easier access and maintenance. Both types of switchboards play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient electricity distribution.</p></p>
