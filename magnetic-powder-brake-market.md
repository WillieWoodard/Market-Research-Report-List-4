<p><p>The magnetic powder brake market consists of two main types: coil rotary and coil stationary. Coil rotary powder brakes are designed with a rotating coil that generates the magnetic field, providing torque control for rotary applications. On the other hand, coil stationary powder brakes have a stationary coil that generates the magnetic field, offering torque control for stationary applications. Both types play a crucial role in various industries where precise torque control is required for machines and equipment.</p></p>
