<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Mass Flow Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Type</li><li>Indirect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct type Mass Flow Controllers are directly installed in the main gas line, and used for precise control of gas flow. They are typically used in applications where accuracy and stability are essential.</p><p>Indirect type Mass Flow Controllers are installed away from the main gas line, and connected via a capillary tube or a valve. They are often used in applications where space is limited or where the gas flow needs to be controlled remotely.</p><p>Both types of Mass Flow Controllers offer precise control of gas flow rates in various industrial applications.</p></p>
