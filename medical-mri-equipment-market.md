<p><ul><li>Magnet System</li><li>RF System</li><li>Computer Imaging System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical MRI equipment market can be divided into three main types: magnet system, RF system, and computer imaging system. The magnet system is responsible for producing the strong magnetic field required for imaging. The RF system generates radiofrequency pulses to stimulate nuclei in the body for imaging. The computer imaging system processes the data collected from the magnet and RF systems to create detailed images of the internal structures of the body. These three components work together seamlessly to produce high-quality MRI images for diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
