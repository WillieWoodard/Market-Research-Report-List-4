<p><p>Medical X-ray generators can be classified into two types: high frequency and low frequency generators. High frequency X-ray generators produce X-rays using high frequency alternating current, allowing for faster image acquisition and higher image quality. They are more efficient and compact compared to low frequency generators. On the other hand, low frequency X-ray generators operate at lower frequencies and are generally larger and less efficient. Both types of generators play a crucial role in medical imaging for diagnosing various conditions.</p></p>
