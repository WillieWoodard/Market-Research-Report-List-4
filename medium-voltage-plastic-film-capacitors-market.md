<p><p>Medium voltage plastic film capacitors are classified into two main types: Polyester Film Capacitors and Metallized Film Capacitors. </p><p>Polyester Film Capacitors use a polyester film as the dielectric material. These capacitors are known for their high insulation resistance and low dielectric absorption, making them suitable for medium voltage applications. </p><p>Metallized Film Capacitors, on the other hand, have a thin metal layer deposited on the film as the electrode. This results in improved capacitance values and high reliability, ideal for medium voltage power systems.</p></p>
