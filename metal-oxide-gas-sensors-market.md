<p><ul><li>Transition-metal Oxides</li><li>Non-transition-metal Oxides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal oxide gas sensors can be classified into two main types based on the type of metal oxide used in the sensor: transition-metal oxides and non-transition-metal oxides. Transition-metal oxides, such as tungsten oxide and titanium oxide, are widely used in gas sensing applications due to their high sensitivity and selectivity. On the other hand, non-transition-metal oxides, including zinc oxide and tin oxide, offer advantages such as low cost and easy fabrication. These two types of metal oxide gas sensors cater to different applications and industries based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
