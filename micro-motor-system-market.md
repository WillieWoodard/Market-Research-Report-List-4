<p><p>The Micro Motor System market includes two main types of motors: Brush and Brushless. Brush motors use brushes to conduct electricity, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness. However, they may require more maintenance due to the brushes wearing out over time. Brushless motors are more efficient, durable, and have higher performance.
