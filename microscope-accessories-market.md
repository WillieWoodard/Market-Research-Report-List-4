Optical-based accessories are commonly used for conventional light microscopy, while Electron-based accessories utilize electron beams for higher resolution imaging. Scanning Probe-based accessories involve the use of a sharp probe to scan surfaces at the nanoscale level. Each type of market offers unique capabilities and applications, catering to different research and industrial needs in the field of microscopy.</p></p>
