<p><p>Night vision camera market is divided into two main types: fixed cameras and PTZ (pan-tilt-zoom) cameras. Fixed cameras are stationary and offer a constant field of view, making them suitable for monitoring specific areas. PTZ cameras, on the other hand, can pan, tilt, and zoom to capture a wider range of areas. Fixed cameras are commonly used in residential security systems, while PTZ cameras are preferred in larger surveillance systems such as in businesses or critical infrastructure sites.</p></p>
