<p><p>Non woven medical textiles are fabrics that are created by bonding or interlocking fibers using mechanical, thermal, or chemical processes, rather than weaving or knitting. One of the key factors driving the market growth is the increasing demand for hygienic and disposable medical products. Non woven medical textiles offer advantages such as cost-effectiveness, breathability, and fluid resistance, which make them ideal for medical applications.</p><p>In addition, the growing awareness about healthcare-associated infections and the need for infection control measures are also driving the demand for non woven medical textiles.
