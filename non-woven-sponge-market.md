<p><p>Non-woven sponges are divided into two main types based on their sterility: sterile non-woven sponges and non-sterile non-woven sponges. Sterile non-woven sponges are individually packaged and designed to be free from any microbial contamination, making them suitable for use in medical procedures where infection control is crucial. Non-sterile non-woven sponges, on the other hand, are not individually packaged and may not be suitable for use in sterile environments, but are commonly used for general cleaning and wound care in non-medical settings.</p></p>
