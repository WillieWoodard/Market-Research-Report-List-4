<p><p>There are two main types of online payroll service markets: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise payroll services require companies to install and maintain software on their own servers, whereas cloud-based services store data on remote servers accessed through the internet. On-premise services offer more control and customization options but require higher upfront costs and maintenance. Cloud-based services are more user-friendly, scalable, and cost-effective as they do not require software installation or hardware maintenance.</p></p>
