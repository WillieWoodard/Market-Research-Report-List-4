<p><p>Optical filters are essential components in various industries, each serving a specific purpose. Band-pass optical filters allow only a certain range of wavelengths to pass through, while high-pass filters transmit wavelengths above a certain threshold. Low-pass filters, on the other hand, only allow wavelengths below a certain threshold to pass through. Band-stop filters block specific wavelengths from passing through. These filters cater to different applications in industries such as telecommunications, healthcare, and spectroscopy.</p></p>
