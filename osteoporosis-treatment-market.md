<p><p>Osteoporosis treatment market offers various types of medications to help manage the condition. Bisphosphonates are commonly prescribed to inhibit bone breakdown and improve bone density. Parathyroid Hormone Therapy stimulates bone formation and reduces the risk of fractures. Calcitonin helps regulate calcium levels in the blood and prevent bone loss. Selective Estrogen Inhibitors Modulator (SERM) mimics the effects of estrogen in the body to prevent bone breakdown. These different types of medications aim to slow down the progression of osteoporosis and reduce the risk of fractures.</p></p>
