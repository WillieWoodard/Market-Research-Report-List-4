<p><p>Oxo-Biodegradable Additive Masterbatch Market can be categorized into two types: Colored Masterbatch and Non-Colored Masterbatch. Colored Masterbatch is a blend of pigments or dyes dispersed in a carrier resin, which is added to plastics to give them a specific color or shade. On the other hand, Non-Colored Masterbatch is a solid additive used for imparting specific properties to plastics, such as UV resistance or anti-static properties, without changing their color. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and sustainability of plastic materials.</p></p>
