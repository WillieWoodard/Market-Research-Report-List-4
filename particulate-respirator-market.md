<p><ul><li>With Breather Valve</li><li>Without Breather Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Particulate respirators are divided into two main types based on the presence of a breather valve. Respirators with a breather valve allow for easier exhalation and reduce heat buildup inside the mask, making them more comfortable for long-term wear. On the other hand, respirators without a breather valve provide more complete filtration but may cause discomfort due to increased exhalation resistance. Both types are widely used in industries where protection against airborne particles is necessary.</p></p>
