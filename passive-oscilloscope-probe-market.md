<p><p>Passive oscilloscope probes come in various types to suit different applications. The 1× probe provides a direct connection to the signal without any attenuation, useful for measuring low-frequency signals. The 10× probe attenuates the signal by a factor of 10, allowing for more accurate measurements of higher frequency signals. The 100× probe attenuates the signal by a factor of 100, suitable for very high-frequency signals. Other types of passive probes may offer different levels of attenuation or other specialized features to meet specific measurement needs.</p></p>
