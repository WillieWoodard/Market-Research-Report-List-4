<p><p>PCB assembly services market types can be categorized into full turnkey, partial turnkey, and kitted/consigned services. Full turnkey services involve the PCB assembler taking care of the entire process from sourcing components to final assembly. Partial turnkey services involve the client providing some components while the assembler supplies the rest. Kitted/consigned services involve the client providing all necessary components and materials for the assembly process. Each market type offers different levels of control and customization for clients based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
