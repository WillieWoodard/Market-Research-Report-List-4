<p><p>Piano DIP switches are available in two main market types: surface-mounting and through-hole mounting. Surface-mounting switches are soldered directly onto the surface of a circuit board, making them compact and ideal for use in small electronic devices. Through-hole mounting switches are inserted into pre-drilled holes on a circuit board and then soldered in place, providing a more secure connection. Both types of switches are widely used in various electronic applications due to their versatility and reliability.</p></p>
