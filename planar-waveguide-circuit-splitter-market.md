<p><p>Planar waveguide circuit splitters come in various types to suit different installation requirements. Bare/Mini type splitters are compact and easy to install directly onto optical fibers. Insertion-Type splitters are designed to be inserted into existing fiber optic networks. Box-Type splitters are enclosed in a protective casing for outdoor or harsh environments. Rack/Tray-Type splitters are designed to be mounted in cabinets or racks for centralized management. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of installation, protection, and accessibility.</p></p>
