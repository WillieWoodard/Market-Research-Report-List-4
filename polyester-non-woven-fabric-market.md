<p><p>Polyester non-woven fabric is widely used in various markets such as spunbond, needle punch, and stitch bond. Spunbond fabric is produced by bonding fibers together through heat and pressure, creating a strong and durable material. Needle punch fabric is made by interlocking fibers through mechanical needles, resulting in a dense and stable fabric. Stitch bond fabric is created by stitching together layers of fibers, providing excellent strength and stability. These types of polyester non-woven fabrics cater to different needs and applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and healthcare.</p></p>
