<p><p>Positive displacement pipettes are commonly categorized into two main types based on their outflow mechanism: Incomplete Outflow Type and Complete Outflow Type. Incomplete Outflow Type pipettes dispense liquid by leaving a small residual volume in the tip, making them suitable for viscous or volatile liquids. On the other hand, Complete Outflow Type pipettes dispense the entire liquid content without any residual volume, making them ideal for accurate and precise dispensing of liquids. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different laboratory requirements.</p></p>
