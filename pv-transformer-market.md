<p><ul><li>Oil Immersed Transformer</li><li>Dry-type Transformer</li><li>Box-type Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PV transformers are essential components in photovoltaic systems, ensuring efficient energy transfer from solar panels to the grid. There are three main types of PV transformers: oil immersed transformers, dry-type transformers, and box-type transformers. Oil immersed transformers use oil as a coolant and insulator, while dry-type transformers do not require oil. Box-type transformers are compact units that provide both step-up and step-down voltage conversions. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of efficiency, safety, and cost-effectiveness for different PV system setups.</p></p>
