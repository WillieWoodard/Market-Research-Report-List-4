<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Radioisotope Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Conversion Type</li><li>No-Thermal Conversion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radioisotope battery market is divided into two types: thermal conversion type and no-thermal conversion type. Thermal conversion type radioisotope batteries generate electricity by converting the heat released from the radioactive decay of isotopes into electrical energy. On the other hand, no-thermal conversion type batteries use the direct emissions of charged particles from the radioisotope decay to produce electricity. Both types of batteries have their advantages and are used in various applications where long-lasting and reliable power sources are needed.</p></p>
