<p><p>Rotary heads are essential components of drilling equipment used in various industries such as construction, mining, and oil and gas. There are primarily two types of rotary heads in the market, hydraulic rotary heads and pneumatic rotary heads. Hydraulic rotary heads use hydraulic pressure to rotate the drill bit, providing higher torque and better efficiency. On the other hand, pneumatic rotary heads use compressed air for rotation, offering a lighter and more cost-effective option. Other types of rotary heads may include electric or mechanical variants, each offering unique features and benefits.</p></p>
