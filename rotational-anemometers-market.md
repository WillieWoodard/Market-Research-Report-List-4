<p><p>Rotational anemometers are devices used to measure wind speed by rotating in response to the air flow. Two common types of rotational anemometers are four-cup and three-cup designs. Four-cup anemometers are typically more accurate and stable at higher wind speeds, making them suitable for professional meteorological applications. On the other hand, three-cup anemometers are simpler in design and more cost-effective, making them popular for personal or hobby use. Both types provide valuable data for a variety of industries and applications.</p></p>
