<p><p>Salmonella testing market includes various types such as molecular diagnostics, monoclonal antibodies, immunoassays, and differential light scattering. Molecular diagnostics involve the detection of specific genetic material of Salmonella bacteria. Monoclonal antibodies are used to identify Salmonella antigens. Immunoassays detect antibodies produced in response to Salmonella infection. Differential light scattering measures changes in light patterns caused by bacterial growth. These different testing methods provide accurate and timely results for the identification of Salmonella in various samples.</p></p>
