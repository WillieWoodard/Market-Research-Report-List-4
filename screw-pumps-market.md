<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Screw Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw Pump</li><li>Twin-Screw Pump</li><li>Multiple Screw Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screw pumps are classified into three main categories: Single screw pumps, Twin-screw pumps, and Multiple screw pumps. Single screw pumps consist of one screw rotating within a cylindrical cavity, providing smooth and pulsation-free flow. Twin-screw pumps have two intermeshing screws that rotate in opposite directions, offering higher capacity and efficiency. Multiple screw pumps have more than two screws, allowing for even higher flow rates and pressure capabilities. Each type of screw pump is suited for different applications and requirements in various industries.</p></p>
