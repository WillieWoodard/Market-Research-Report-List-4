<p><p>Seawater Reverse Osmosis System market consists of two main types - One-way Reverse Osmosis and Two-way Reverse Osmosis. One-way Reverse Osmosis systems only produce fresh water by removing salts and impurities from seawater, making it suitable for consumption. On the other hand, Two-way Reverse Osmosis systems are designed to produce both fresh water and brine concentrate, allowing for more efficient water desalination processes. Both types play a crucial role in providing clean drinking water in areas where freshwater sources are limited.</p></p>
