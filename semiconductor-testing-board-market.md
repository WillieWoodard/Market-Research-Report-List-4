<p><p>Semiconductor testing boards are essential components in the manufacturing process of semiconductors. There are three main types of testing boards: Probe Cards, Load Boards, and Burn-in Boards. Probe cards are used for testing the electrical characteristics of individual semiconductor devices. Load boards are used to test the entire system or device. Burn-in boards are used for stressing the device under extreme conditions to ensure its reliability. Each type of testing board plays a critical role in ensuring the quality and functionality of semiconductor products.</p></p>
