<p><ul><li>Hard CMP Pads</li><li>Soft CMP Pads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor wafer polishing pads are categorized into two main types: hard CMP pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are known for their durability and ability to maintain consistent pressure during the polishing process, making them ideal for achieving precise and uniform results. On the other hand, soft CMP pads are more flexible and conformable, allowing for better planarization and surface finish. Both types have their advantages and are used based on specific requirements of the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
