<p><p>Semiconductor wafer vacuum heaters are essential equipment in the semiconductor manufacturing process. There are two main types of vacuum heaters in the market - single zone vacuum heaters and multi-zone vacuum heaters. Single zone vacuum heaters are designed to heat a single area of the semiconductor wafer, while multi-zone vacuum heaters can heat multiple areas simultaneously. Single zone heaters are more common and cost-effective for smaller scale operations, while multi-zone heaters offer more precise and uniform heating for larger scale production processes.</p></p>
