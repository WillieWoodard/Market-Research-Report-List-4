<p><p>The Smart Bathroom Cabinet market consists of two main types: Wall Cabinets and Floor Cabinets. Wall Cabinets are mounted on the wall above the sink or toilet, utilizing the vertical space in the bathroom. These cabinets provide storage without taking up floor space. On the other hand, Floor Cabinets are placed directly on the floor, offering both storage space and additional surface space for bathroom essentials. Both types of cabinets come equipped with smart features such as LED lights, built-in Bluetooth speakers, and touchless operation for a more convenient bathroom experience.</p></p>
