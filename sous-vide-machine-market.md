<p><p>Sous Vide Machines come in two main types: Immersion Types and Water Bath Types. Immersion Types are handheld devices that attach to a pot or container filled with water, while Water Bath Types are standalone machines with a built-in water bath. Immersion Types are more affordable and convenient for home use, while Water Bath Types are larger and often preferred by professional chefs and restaurants. Both types offer precise temperature control and consistent results for sous vide cooking.</p></p>
