<p><ul><li>Channel Mixers</li><li>Pipe Mixers</li><li>Gas Dispersion Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Static mixers are commonly categorized into three main types: channel mixers, pipe mixers, and gas dispersion systems. Channel mixers consist of alternating plates or baffles within a channel to mix fluids efficiently. Pipe mixers incorporate elements such as baffles or twisted elements within the pipe to achieve mixing. Gas dispersion systems are designed to mix gases into liquids by creating small bubbles to enhance contact between the two phases. Each type of static mixer offers unique advantages and is used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
