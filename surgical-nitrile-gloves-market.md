<p><p>Surgical nitrile gloves are available in two main types: powdered gloves and non-powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with a fine powder, usually cornstarch, to make them easier to put on and remove. Non-powdered gloves do not have this coating, making them suitable for individuals with latex allergies. Both types offer protection against contaminants and are commonly used in medical and surgical settings. The choice between powdered and non-powdered gloves often depends on personal preference and specific workplace requirements.</p></p>
