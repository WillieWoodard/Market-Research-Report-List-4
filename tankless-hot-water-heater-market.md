<p><p>A tankless hot water heater is a type of water heating system that heats water on demand, rather than storing it in a tank. This results in hot water being available instantly, without the need for a large storage tank. Tankless hot water heaters are more energy efficient compared to traditional tank water heaters, as they only heat water when needed, leading to lower energy consumption and utility bills.</p><p>The tankless hot water heater market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 11.9% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in technology have led to the development of more efficient and compact tankless water heaters, further driving market growth.</p><p>The latest trends in the tankless hot water heater market include the integration of smart technologies, such as WiFi connectivity and mobile apps for remote control, as well as the development of hybrid tankless water heaters that combine the benefits of a tankless system with a small storage tank. These trends are expected to continue shaping the market landscape in the coming years.</p></p>
