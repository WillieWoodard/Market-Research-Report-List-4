Mid Power Thin-Disc Lasers have an output ranging from 100 watts to 500 watts and find applications in materials processing and medical devices. High Power Thin-Disc Lasers have an output exceeding 500 watts and are utilized in industrial cutting, welding, and drilling applications.</p></p>
