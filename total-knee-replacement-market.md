Primary Knee Systems are designed for patients who require full knee replacement due to severe arthritis or injury. These systems replace the entire knee joint with artificial components. Partial Knee Systems, on the other hand, are used for patients with localized arthritis, only replacing the damaged part of the knee joint. Both types of systems aim to restore function, reduce pain, and improve quality of life for patients with knee issues.</p></p>
