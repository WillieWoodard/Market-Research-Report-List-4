<p><p>The Upper Arm Blood Pressure Monitor market consists of two main types: Mercury Blood Pressure Monitor and Digital Blood Pressure Monitor. Mercury monitors use a column of mercury to measure blood pressure, providing accurate readings but requiring calibration and maintenance. Digital monitors use electronic technology to provide quick and convenient readings without the use of mercury. Both types have their own advantages and drawbacks, with the market shifting towards digital monitors due to their ease of use and portability.</p></p>
