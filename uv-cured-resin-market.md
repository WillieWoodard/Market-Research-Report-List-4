<p><strong>The UV Cured Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oligomers</li><li>Monomers</li><li>Photoinitiators</li><li>Additives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV cured resin market can be segmented into oligomers, monomers, photoinitiators, and additives. Oligomers are pre-polymers that provide the backbone structure of the cured material. Monomers are small molecules that react with oligomers to form the polymer network. Photoinitiators are chemicals that start the curing process when exposed to UV light. Additives are ingredients added to enhance the performance or properties of the final cured resin. Each of these components plays a crucial role in the overall performance and characteristics of the UV cured resin.</p></p>
