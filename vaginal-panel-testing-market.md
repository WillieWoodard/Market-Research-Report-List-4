<p><ul><li>Direct Molecular DNA Probe</li><li>Molecular Nucleic Acid Amplification Test (NAAT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vaginal panel testing involves two main types of testing methods: Direct Molecular DNA Probe and Molecular Nucleic Acid Amplification Test (NAAT). Direct Molecular DNA Probe testing directly detects genetic material of specific pathogens in vaginal samples. Molecular Nucleic Acid Amplification Test (NAAT) is a highly sensitive method that amplifies and detects nucleic acids of pathogens in vaginal samples. Both types of testing provide accurate and reliable results for diagnosing various infections in the vaginal region.</p></p>
