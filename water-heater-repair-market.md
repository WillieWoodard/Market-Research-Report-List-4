<p><p>The water heater repair market can be divided into two main types: Tank Storage and Tankless. Tank Storage water heaters store and heat water in a large tank, which can require repairs related to leaks, corrosion, or heating element issues. On the other hand, Tankless water heaters heat water on demand, eliminating the need for a storage tank but potentially requiring repairs for issues like scale build-up or sensor malfunctions. Both markets offer opportunities for repair services to address the specific needs of each type of water heater.</p></p>
