Immersed aerators are placed underwater and draw in water through a pump mechanism to aerate it. Surface aerators create a fountain-like effect to introduce oxygen into the water. Other types of aerators include diffused air systems and mechanical aerators. Each type of aerator is designed to effectively aerate water in different settings such as wastewater treatment plants, lakes, and ponds.</p></p>
