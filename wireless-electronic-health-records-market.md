<p><p>Wireless Electronic Health Records Market Types include various technologies such as 3G/commercial cellular, Bluetooth, Radio Frequency Identification (RFID), Satellites, Sensors, WiMAX, Wireless LANs, and Zigbee. 3G/commercial cellular networks provide reliable communication for EHR data transfer. Bluetooth allows for short-range wireless connections between devices. RFID technology enables data tracking and identification. Satellites enable remote access and data transfer for healthcare providers. Sensors collect patient data for monitoring. WiMAX technology offers high-speed internet connectivity. Wireless LANs provide local network connections. Zigbee technology facilitates communication between devices in healthcare settings.</p></p>
