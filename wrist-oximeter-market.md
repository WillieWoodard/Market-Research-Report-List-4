No Fingertip Probe Type wrist oximeters do not require a separate probe to be attached to the finger, making them more convenient and comfortable for users. On the other hand, Fingertip Probe Type wrist oximeters have a separate probe that needs to be attached to the fingertip for accurate readings. Both types serve the same purpose of monitoring oxygen levels in the blood.</p></p>
